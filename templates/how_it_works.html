Getting Started

Your Step‑by‑Step Guide to Bank of SA

Follow these simple steps to set up your account and start using all the smart banking features – from instant transfers to goal‑based savings.

1

Create Your Account

Click Register and fill in your details. You'll need:

  • Full name
  • South African ID number (13 digits)
  • Email address and phone number
  • A secure password

Our system automatically verifies your ID and checks that you're 18+.

Tip: Use a valid ID format – the system validates South African ID numbers.
2

Log In to Your Dashboard

Use your email and password to log in. Once logged in, you'll see your Dashboard – the central hub of your banking activity.

From here you can:

  • View your account balance
  • See recent transactions
  • Access all banking features
Security: Your password is hashed with BCrypt – we never store plain text passwords.
3

Explore Your Dashboard

Your dashboard shows a snapshot of your finances:

  • Balance – total across all accounts
  • Recent Transactions – last 10 activities
  • Savings Goals – progress on your goals
  • Notifications – important alerts

Use the navigation bar to quickly jump to any feature.

4

Send Money – The Transfer Feature

To experience the full power of transfers, you need two accounts. Here's how:

Important

Since this is a demo, you can create a second account with any fake credentials (different email). Use a second browser or incognito window to log in to both accounts simultaneously – then you can transfer money between them in real time!

Once you have two accounts:

  1. Log in to Account A, go to Transfer.
  2. Enter Account B's 10‑digit account number.
  3. Enter an amount and a description (optional).
  4. Review the fee (1%, min R2, max R100) and click Transfer Money.
  5. The balance of Account A decreases, and Account B's balance increases instantly.

You can also undo a transfer within 15 minutes – a unique safety net!

Real‑time updates: Both accounts' balances and transaction histories update immediately. You'll see the transfer reflected on both dashboards in real time.
More Features

Beyond Transfers

Deposit

Add funds to your main account. Deposits are recorded instantly and appear in your transaction history.

Savings Goals

Set a target amount and deadline, then deposit money into a dedicated goal account. Track your progress with a visual bar.

Bill Payments

Pay electricity, water, airtime, and data bundles directly from your account. All payments are processed instantly.

5

Stay Informed with Notifications & History

Every transaction triggers a notification – you'll always know what's happening with your money.

  • Notifications: Get alerts for deposits, transfers, bill payments, and low balance warnings.
  • Transaction History: View a complete log of all your activities, with filters by type and search.
  • Undo: If you made a mistake, you can reverse a transfer within 15 minutes – just click the "Undo" button next to the transaction.
